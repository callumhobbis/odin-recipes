Chocolate Cake

Description

This is my favourite chocolate cake recipe: lovely and rich, and made in a slightly unusual way. Yes, it really does need granulated sugar, boiling water, and oil!

Ingredients

Cake Batter

  • 1 ¾ cups (225 g) plain white flour
  • 1 ¾ cups (350 g) granulated sugar
  • ¾ cup (67 g) cocoa powder
  • 1 ½ tsp baking powder
  • 1 ½ tsp bicarbonate of soda
  • ½ tsp salt
  • 2 eggs
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) vanilla essence
  • ½ cup (125 ml) oil (e.g. canola)
  • 1 cup (250 ml) milk
  • 1 cup (250 ml) boiling water

Basic Chocolate Icing

  • 1 ½ cups (202 g) icing sugar
  • 1 ½ tbsp (9 g) cocoa powder
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) butter, melted
  • 1 tsp (5 ml) vanilla essence
  • 1–3 tbsp (15–45 ml) water

Steps

Cake

  1. Preheat oven to 160°C (320°F).
  2. Prepare a 25 × 25 cm cake tin, spray well with cooking spray or line.
  3. In a large bowl, add the flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, bicarbonate of soda, and salt, and mix well until fully combined.
  4. Add this to the eggs, vanilla, oil, and milk, and blend well.
  5. Finally, add the boiling water and carefully stir until completely blended together.
  6. Pour into your prepared cake tin and bae for 35–45 minutes (all tin sizes differ, so check often after 30 minutes).

Icing

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the icing sugar and cocoa powder and mix well.
  2. Add to this the melted butter and vanilla essence and stir well.
  3. Add water a little at a time until you have your desired consistency.
  4. Spread generously over the cake, allow to set, slice, and serve!
  5. Store in an airtight container.
