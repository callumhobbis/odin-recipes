Cookie in a Mug

Description

A cookie in a mug recipe that can be done in under 10 minutes! Perfect for when you want some baked goodness but can't be bothered to make a whole batch of cookies. I've made this one so many times!

Ingredients

  • 1 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1 tbsp granulated sugar
  • 1 tbsp brown sugar, firmly packed
  • ½ tsp vanilla extract
  • ⅛ tsp (a pinch of) salt
  • 1 egg yolk
  • 3 tbsp plain flour
  • 1–2 tbsp chocolate chips

Steps

  1. Microwave butter in a mug until melted (30 seconds to 1 minute).
  2. With a spoon, mix in the sugars, vanilla extract, and salt until well-combined.
  3. Mix in the egg yolk until no traces of the yolk are seen.
  4. Mix in the flour.
  5. Fold in the chocolate chips.
  6. Microwave on high for 40–50 seconds.
  7. Serve warm (maybe even with a dollop of ice cream).
