Spring Onion Frittelle

Description

These spring onion pancakes are so quick to make and perfect for a snack. Dip them in whatever sauce you like, but my favourite is sweet chilli. Try substituting some of the spring onions with whatever veg you have left around!

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • ½ cup plain flour
  • 2 tsp baking powder
  • ½ cup water
  • ¼ cup grated cheese
  • salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 ½ cups (2 bunches) spring onions
  • 1 green chilli, minced (optional)
  • 2 tbsp olive oil

Steps

  1. In a medium-sized bowl, combine the eggs, flour, baking powder, water, cheese, salt, and pepper, and whisk well to combine.
  2. Stir the spring onions and green chillies into the batter.
  3. In a nonstick or cast iron pan, heat 2 tablespoons of olive oil.
  4. Drop by tablespoonfuls into the hot pan to make 2 inch pancakes.
  5. Cook until golden brown on the first side then flip to brown on the other side.
  6. Remove from the oil with a slotted spoon and set on a plate lined with paper towels to drain.
  7. Repeat with the remaining batter.
  8. Serve immediately.
